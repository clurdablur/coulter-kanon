Riley Bourgault

Maid of Honor

It all started in third grade. When I first met Riley, I thought she was weird. One day her (intimidating at the time) dad visited her at lunch and insisted that we be friends. I was so scared that I decided to give our friendship a try. Let's just say we became sisters. We lived on the same street and practically grew up living together. We spent countless days cruising on the golf cart, playing club penguin and roller coaster tycoon, and jamming out to music in her 1965 mustang that would randomly break down on the street while she reassured me that it happens and we would be okay. I am so glad her father forced me to be her friend!

Bridesmaid
